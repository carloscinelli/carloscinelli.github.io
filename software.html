Regression Sensitivity Analysis: the Robustness Value and the Partial R2

Shiny App

The Shiny App "Regression Sensitivity Analysis: the Robustness Value and the Partial R2"allows researchers to examine how sensitive their regression estimates are to the inclusion of unobserved confounders. It also allows bounding the strength of unobserved confounders based on comparisons to observed covariates. These results do not rely on any assumptions regarding the functional form of the treatment assignment mechanism nor on the distribution of the unobserved confounders, and hold for multiple confounders, possibly acting non-linearly. The Shiny App uses the R package sensemakr and is based on the results of Cinelli and Hazlett (2020).

sensemakr: Sensitivity Analysis Tools for OLS

R package

The R package sensemakr implements a suite of sensitivity analysis tools that extends the traditional omitted variable bias framework and makes it easier to understand the impact of omitted variables in regression models, as discussed in Cinelli and Hazlett (2020).
