About

My methodological research focuses in Statistics and Causal Inference. Real world data is marred by several biases, and I develop new methods and tools for helping scientists make more transparent and more credible causal inferences. You can find a list with my recent publications here.

Short Bio: Carlos Cinelli is a PhD candidate in Statistics at the University of California, Los Angeles. Prior to his doctoral studies, Carlos obtained a masters in Economics at the University of Brasilia (2012) and worked as an Economist at the Central Bank of Brazil (2012-2016).
